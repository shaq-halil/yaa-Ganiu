Welcome to Yaa Ganiu Foundation

Empowering young lives through mentorship, education, and holistic care. Together, we’re shaping futures, unlocking potential, and transforming communities—one life at a time.

Our Mission

At Yaa Ganiu Foundation, we believe that everyone regardless of background—deserves the chance to discover their true potential. Our mission is to guide talented youth away from the dangers of the street and toward a path of purpose, growth, and opportunity.

Our Programs

We provide comprehensive support through four key programs designed to address the fundamental needs of vulnerable children.

Education

Providing quality education, school supplies, and tutoring to ensure every child can learn and grow.

Shelter

Safe, nurturing homes where children can feel secure and develop healthy relationships.

Healthcare

Comprehensive medical care, nutrition programs, and mental health support for holistic well-being.

Mentorship

One-on-one guidance and life skills training to help children build confidence and independence.
